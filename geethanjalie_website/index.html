Why You Should Embrace Carnatic Music

Rich Musical Heritage

Experience a musical tradition that dates back centuries, filled with intricate ragas and soulful compositions.

Deep Learning & Insight

Our classes delve into the theory and practice of Carnatic music, helping you master ragas, talas, and improvisation.

Live Performances

Showcase your progress through live performances and collaborative concerts that bring the tradition to life.

Community & Culture

Join a vibrant community of music enthusiasts and experts, and be a part of cultural events and festivals.

GEETHAANJALIE School of Carnatic Music, founded in 2008 in Melbourne by Ahilan Sivanadan, teaches Carnatic vocal and violin. Students participate in concerts, temple events, and cultural programs, collaborating with music and dance schools.
